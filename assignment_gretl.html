Step A

1. Research Question

The purpose of this paper is to investigate factors affecting student performance. The research question is:

How are reading and writing scores associated with mathematics performance among students?

2. Data Set

The dataset used in this analysis is the Students Performance dataset from Kaggle. The dataset contains information about gender, parental education, lunch type, test preparation course, and student exam scores in mathematics, reading, and writing.

5. Model Selection and Analysis

The different models were compared based on explanatory power and theoretical relevance. The multiple regression and non-linear models provide improved model fit compared to the simple regression model.

Based on the results, the preferred model is the multiple regression model because it captures more information relevant to mathematics performance.

The preferred model can be used to predict mathematics scores for students with different reading and writing performance levels.

Conclusion

The analysis indicates a positive relationship between reading performance and mathematics performance. The results suggest that reading and writing skills are important predictors of student outcomes in mathematics.

Overall, the multiple regression model provided the most useful explanation of student mathematics performance in this dataset.
